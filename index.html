Perseverance Through Words: A Tribute to Christy Nolan

The “Perseverance Through Words” competition celebrates the legacy of Christy Nolan and empowers disabled students and staff at Trinity College Dublin to showcase their creativity. Through storytelling, poetry, and personal reflections, participants share their unique perspectives and demonstrate the resilience at the heart of Christy Nolan's inspirational life.

This tribute encourages self-expression and inclusivity, echoing Christy Nolan's belief that creativity knows no bounds. Join us in commemorating Christy Nolan's achievements and celebrating the voices of the disabled community.
