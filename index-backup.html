Art for Action

Building an Inclusive Future!

This is the fourth year of Trinity's DisAbility Art Exhibition, entitled "Art for Action: Building an Inclusive Future!" The exhibition will run from Monday 2nd through to Friday 6th December in An Mheitheal, Trinity disAbility Hub, Printing House Square. A huge thank you to the Trinity disAbility Service, as well as Declan Treanor (DS Director) and Odhrán Mc Laughlin (Project Administrator), for their help and support with the exhibition. We'd like to thank all the artists who submitted their extraordinary and thought-provoking work!

Efforts to promote inclusion must go beyond the ephemerality of one-time events, striving instead for lasting systemic changes that ensure accessibility and equality for people with disabilities.
